Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Please review what happens when you delete your account.

warning

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Some data may be retained as required by law.

What You'll Lose

person_off

Profile & Account

Your profile, username, and account settings will be permanently deleted.

receipt_long

Order History

All past orders, receipts, and transaction records will be erased.

star_off

Reviews & Ratings

Your reviews, ratings, and all content you've contributed will be removed.

loyalty

Rewards & Credits

Any unused credits, rewards points, or promotional balances will be forfeited.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open the Pusheat App

Navigate to Settings from your profile tab.

2

Go to Account Settings

Scroll down and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom.

3

Confirm Your Identity

Enter your password or verify via email to confirm.

4

Confirm Deletion

Review the summary and confirm. Your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Ready to proceed?

You can also request account deletion by emailing us.
