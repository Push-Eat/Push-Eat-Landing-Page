FAQ

Questions

Where is Pusheat available?+

We are live in Lagos, Nigeria. New cities will follow soon.

How do I order?+

Pick a deal, choose delivery or pickup, add your details, and pay from your wallet. All inside the app.

How do deals work?+

Join a deal and pay from your wallet. If it hits its target, the creator cooks. If it does not, your payment goes back to your wallet.

How do creators earn on Pusheat?+

Creators post dishes, set prices and schedules, and cook on their terms. We handle delivery and payouts.

When will my meal be ready?+

Each deal shows the prep and delivery window before you pay. Pickup availability is also shown.

What if a deal fails or I cancel?+

If a deal does not reach its target, your payment returns to your wallet. Same if you cancel within the allowed window.
